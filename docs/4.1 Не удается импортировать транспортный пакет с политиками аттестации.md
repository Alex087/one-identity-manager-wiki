4.1 Не удается импортировать транспортный пакет с политиками аттестации

29 августа 2022 г.

16:55

 

Нужно импортировать объект

AttestationObjectHasPWODM

 

*You need to insert into AttestationObjectHasPWODM table manually for
one time. You insert your attestation procedure UID
(UID_AttestationObject) and your workflow UID (UID_PWODecisionMethod).
You can click on the column and find your related Attestationprocedure
and Workflow. After that process you can create your attestation policy
easily without no error.*
